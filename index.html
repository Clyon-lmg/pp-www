PP Peptide Planner

Plan injections. Track doses. Know when to reorder.

Peptide Planner helps you run clean cycles with flexible schedules (weekdays, custom days, or every N days), log each dose, and see your next reorder date automatically. Inventory ties to your schedule, so you never run out mid‑cycle.

Custom Schedules

Pick specific weekdays, run 5/2 or 3×/week, or set “every N days”. Calendar shows upcoming injections and the home screen only lists today’s items.

Inventory‑Aware Reordering

Enter mg per vial and BAC to compute mg/mL. Peptide Planner counts remaining doses and estimates the reorder date using your supplier’s lead time.

Vendor Peek

During Add, we suggest vendors (when pricing is available), ranked by $/mg and aware of coupons. One click sets default supplier and lead time.

Privacy

Your protocol data belongs to you. We use minimal analytics to improve the product and never sell personal data. Email is used for login and account notifications.
